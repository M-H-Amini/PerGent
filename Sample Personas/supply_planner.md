# Supply Planner

![Supply Planner avatar](avatar.png)

## Jobs to be Done

- **J1** Forecast demand for key stock keeping units (SKU)
- **J2** Assess supplier delay impact on service levels
- **J3** Reallocate inventory across regions
- **J4** Balance supply plans with production capacity
- **J5** Build weekly supply plans with constraints
- **J6** Prioritize orders during shortages
- **J7** Coordinate expediting actions with suppliers
- **J8** Track inventory health (days of supply, excess, obsolete)
- **J9** Align launch plans with marketing and sales
- **J10** Report plan risks and mitigation actions

## Pain Points

- **P1** Supplier updates arrive late or inconsistently
- **P2** Fragmented demand and inventory data across teams
- **P3** Difficult to explain planning changes to stakeholders
- **P4** Frequent last-minute demand spikes disrupt plans
- **P5** Manual data cleanup across spreadsheets each cycle
- **P6** Limited visibility to in-transit inventory
- **P7** Conflicting priorities from sales and operations
- **P8** System latency during planning cycles
- **P9** Slow approvals for reallocations and exceptions
- **P10** KPI definitions vary by region and team

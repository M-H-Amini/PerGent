# Demand Planner

![Demand Planner avatar](avatar.png)

## Jobs to be Done

- **J1** Generate accurate short- and mid-term demand forecasts
- **J2** Capture promotions, cannibalization, and seasonality effects
- **J3** Collaborate with sales and marketing on demand signals
- **J4** Maintain consensus forecast and manage versioning
- **J5** Perform demand sensing using POS and market data
- **J6** Quantify forecast error and root-cause analysis
- **J7** Shape promotional and assortment plans with insights
- **J8** Communicate demand risks and recommended mitigations

## Pain Points

- **P1** Incomplete or delayed point-of-sale and retail data
- **P2** Poor visibility into promotional effectiveness
- **P3** Misalignment between commercial and planning teams
- **P4** Overfitting forecasts to short-term noise
- **P5** Lack of reliable baseline for new product forecasting
- **P6** Limited tooling for rapid what-if experimentation

## Skills

- **S1** Time series forecasting and demand-sensing techniques
- **S2** Data analysis and feature engineering for demand drivers
- **S3** Cross-functional collaboration with sales and marketing
- **S4** Experiment design and A/B analysis for promotions
- **S5** Statistical validation and forecast error analysis
- **S6** Storytelling with data for stakeholder alignment
- **S7** Product lifecycle demand management
- **S8** Tooling automation and dashboarding

## Tools

- **T1** Point-of-sale (POS) and retailer reporting feeds
- **T2** Statistical forecasting platforms or libraries
- **T3** Demand planning / S&OP collaboration tools
- **T4** Promotion planning and lift-analysis tools
- **T5** Business intelligence dashboards and notebooks
- **T6** CRM and marketing campaign systems
- **T7** Data lake / ETL pipelines for ingestion
- **T8** Versioned models and experiment tracking
